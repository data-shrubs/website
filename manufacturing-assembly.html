Manufacturing & Assembly

Quick turnaround time, flexibility in volumes and reduced cost!

Overview

Datashrubs offers premium fabrication and assembly services with the fastest turnaround time in India with the help of our manufacturing partners, We can handle prototype orders as minimum as one (1) to one hundred thousand (100,000) boards with respect to volumes manufacturing. We are specialised in high density surface mount PCB fab & assembly functions. We do PCB manufacturing and assembly inside the same facility which leads to reduced cost and effective TAT in the business.
